Welcome -To My Journey

I started my coding journey when I was in First Year of my Engineering But that time I was not consistent at all and used to study programming languages only for the exam purpose. Soon within the bling of an eye I got Graduated in the year 2020. Somehow I managed to get placed in the company. But didn't got the role which I always wished for.I wanted to be a Developer but as it was service based company they just thrown me randomly in support role.Then one of my Friend informed me about Tanay Pratap Sir. She insisted me to complete the Neog-camp.
Gradually,I started seeing the videos of Level-zero. While seeing the videos ,I used to follow each an every step suggested by Tanay Sir.I used to perform Hands-on after watching every video.This developed confidence in me and I thoroughly started enjoying the learning and the process of coding.Then slowly I started building small projects.
Thanks to Tanay Sir for helping me to gain my Confidence back.
